About Me

I am currently an assistant professor in the Institute of Statistics and Big Data at the Renmin University of China. My main research interests include federated learning, and combining the mixture models with the optimal transportation to solve various machine learning problems.

I received my Ph.D. degree in Statistics (2022) and M.Sc. degree in Statistics (2017) from the University of British Columbia under the supervision of Professor Jiahua Chen, and my B.Sc. degree in Statistics (2015) from the School of the Gifted Young (SCGY) at the University of Science and Technology of China.

About Me

I am currently an associate professor in the Department of Mathematics at Shantou University. My main research interest focuses on statistical modeling of air pollution data and quantitatively analyzing its impact on human health.

I received my Ph.D. degree in Statistics (2016) from the University of Glasgow under the supervision of Professors Duncan Lee, and Marian Scott.
